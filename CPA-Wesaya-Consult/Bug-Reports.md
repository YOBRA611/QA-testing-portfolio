**CPA Wesaya Consult QA audit**

**Focus:** Appointment Booking & Contact Form

**CPA-001:** Appointment Booking: No confirmation on mobile

**Severity:** 🟠 Medium

**Description:** On mobile devices, after clicking "Book Appointment," the page does not scroll up to show the success message.

**Actual Result:** Users keep clicking "Submit" multiple times because they don't think it worked, creating duplicate entries.

**Expected Result:** Page should auto-scroll to the success notification.

**CPA-002:** Contact Form: "Inquiry Type" dropdown is not mandatory

**Severity:** 🟡 Low

**Description:** The dropdown for "Tax," "Audit," or "Consulting" defaults to blank and is not a required field.

**Actual Result:** The CPA receives emails without knowing what service the client needs, slowing down the response time.
